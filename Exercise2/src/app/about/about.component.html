Understanding the Spread

In the NFL the best team will win the vast majority of the games. Spread betting is the most popular NFL betting option because spread betting handicaps the favourite team, which puts both teams on a more even playing field. Therefore, betters have more of a 50/50 shot to pick the correct team, even if its the non-favorited team. Below is an example

  • Let's say we are going to bet on the Eagles V.S. the Browns game
  • In this game specifically, let's say the browns have been terrible for years now, while the Eagles have been winning many games consecutively
  • Eagles would obviously be the favorites against the browns. So the Eagles will be given a handicap to make it more of a even matchup between the two teams.
  • Let's say the Eagles are handicapped -7.5 points, this means that the eagles must win by 7.5 points or more in order to win the bet. Since, we can't score .5 points in football, the Eagles must win by 8 or more.
  • If the Eagles are handicapped, -7.5 points against the browns then they browns should be given +7.5 points to create an even matchup.This also makes it so that you can only pick one or the other.
  • In order to calculate we should add the points to the underdogs final score and whoever has more points after that, wins the bet
    • the eagles are -7.5 favorites over the browns
    • So if the Eagles won 34-30, we would add 7.5 to the browns final score, resulting in a score of winning 37.5-34, and in turn whoever bet on the browns wins their bet as well
    • If the Eagles won 28-20, we would add 7.5 to the browns final score again, resulting in the browns losing 27.5-28, so bettors of the Eagles would win.
    • Lastly, if Browns won 18-15, bettors would also win the bet since after we add the spread to the browns score, we get 25.5-15, in favor of the browns.
  • Hopefully this Explains how spread betting works, its easy to understand with some practice.
